日本音楽FEST

Welcome to Nippon Ongaku Fest — a celebration of the vibrant soundscape of modern Japan. Join us for an immersive musical journey featuring some of the most iconic voices and rising talents of the Japanese music scene. From powerful live performances to unforgettable cultural experiences, our festival brings the spirit, energy, and creativity of Japan to the stage. Step into a world where music connects us all.

EXPERIENCE

Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Nippon Ongaku Fest — where music, culture, and nature come together in perfect harmony. Feel the energy of live performances by top Japanese artists, surrounded by the stunning mountain scenery of Yuzawa. Explore food stalls offering local Niigata delicacies, enjoy interactive workshops, and connect with fans who share your passion for Japanese music and culture. From sunrise beats to night performances under the stars, every moment at the festival is a celebration of creativity, joy, and connection.
