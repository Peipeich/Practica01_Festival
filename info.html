MAP

Location: Naeba Ski Resort, 202 Mikuni, Yuzawa, Minamiuonuma District, Niigata 949-6212, Japón

  • Train:Take the Joetsu Shinkansen from Tokyo to Echigo-Yuzawa Station — the trip takes about 80 minutes. From the station, it's just a short taxi or local bus ride to Mikuni.
  • Bus:Highway buses from major cities like Tokyo and Niigata offer comfortable and affordable access to Yuzawa.
  • Car:Drive along the Kan-Etsu Expressway and exit at Yuzawa I.C. to enjoy a scenic route through the mountains of Niigata.
